Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 2026

How to Request Data Deletion

If you have interacted with Cardynal through Facebook, Instagram, or any other connected platform, you can request the deletion of your data at any time.

To request deletion of your data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to privacy@cardynal.io with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include the email address or identifier associated with your account or interaction (e.g., your Facebook profile name or page you contacted).
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request within 48 hours.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete the following data associated with your interactions:

  • Conversation history and messages
  • Contact information collected during interactions
  • Any analytics data linked to your identifier

Processing Time

Your data deletion request will be processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Additional Information

For more details about how we collect and handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, contact us at privacy@cardynal.io.
