Questions?
Let's clear things up.

AI-powered solutions, business automation tools... a lot of big words can get confusing. We're here to make it clear.

What exactly is Cardynal?

Cardynal is a fully managed AI support platform. We deploy intelligent AI agents that handle customer conversations across WhatsApp, web chat, and all your channels. You get a complete solution — AI dashboard (app.cardynal.io) and help desk (chat.cardynal.io) — without building anything yourself.

How is this different from Zendesk, Intercom, or Freshdesk?

Those are help desks with AI features bolted on. You still need to set them up, integrate them, and maintain them. Cardynal is AI-first and fully managed — we deploy everything for you and optimize it continuously. Plus, our WhatsApp integration takes 30 seconds, not 30 days.

How does the WhatsApp QR integration work?

Just like WhatsApp Web. You scan a QR code with your phone, and your WhatsApp is connected. No Meta Business API approval, no new phone number, no waiting. Your existing WhatsApp account becomes AI-powered instantly. Both options support the same features and enterprise-grade security.

Do I need technical skills to use Cardynal?

No. We handle all the technical work. You use the dashboard to monitor conversations, review analytics, and make business decisions. If you want to tweak playbooks yourself, the interface is intuitive — but we're always here to help.

What channels do you support?

WhatsApp (via QR scan), WhatsApp API, web chat widget, Instagram, Facebook, X (Twitter), Telegram, Discord, LinkedIn, and API for custom integrations. Email and SMS are coming soon.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a proof-of-concept phase where you can test with real conversations before committing. If it's not working for you, you walk away — no long-term contracts required.

How fast can I go live?

48 hours from kickoff to live. Most of that is us building your playbooks and loading your knowledge base. The technical deployment takes minutes.

How do I add the chat widget to my website?

We provide a WordPress plugin that installs in one click. Activate it, and paste your key. The chat widget appears instantly on your site. Not on WordPress? We also offer a simple JavaScript snippet for any website.
