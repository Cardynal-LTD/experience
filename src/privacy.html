Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Cardynal ("we", "us", "our") operates the Cardynal customer support automation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

By using Cardynal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, company name, and billing information.

Conversation Data

We process customer conversations that flow through your Cardynal-powered support channels. This may include names, contact details, and any information customers share during support interactions.

Usage Data

We collect data about how you use our platform, including login times, features accessed, and performance metrics.

Technical Data

We automatically collect IP addresses, browser type, device information, and cookies to ensure platform functionality and security.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Process customer support conversations through AI
  • Generate analytics and reports for your dashboard
  • Send important service updates
  • Improve our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Processing and AI

Cardynal uses artificial intelligence to process customer conversations. See our AI Transparency page for details on how our AI handles data.

We do not use your data to train AI models.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • LLM Providers — To process AI conversations (data is not retained by providers for training)
  • Infrastructure Providers — For hosting and service delivery
  • Payment Processors — To handle billing
  • Legal Authorities — When required by law

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Conversation data is retained according to your plan settings. You can request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

Under Israel's Protection of Privacy Law (Amendment 13, 2025), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@cardynal.io

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. See our Security page for details.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside Israel. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers.

Children's Privacy

Cardynal is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.
