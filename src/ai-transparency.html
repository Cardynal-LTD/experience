How Cardynal AI Works

Cardynal uses large language models (LLMs) to power conversational AI agents that handle customer support inquiries. Our AI understands context, retrieves relevant information from your knowledge base, and responds in natural language — including native Hebrew.

When the AI detects complex situations, emotional distress, or topics outside its scope, it automatically escalates to your human team with full conversation context.

Your Data

We do not train AI models on your data. Customer conversations are processed in real-time to generate responses, but they are never used to train or fine-tune any language models.

Data stays in your control. You can access, export, or delete your conversation data at any time.

Where data is processed. Conversations are processed through secure API connections to LLM providers. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3.

Transparency to End Users

Your customers know they're talking to AI. Our interface clearly identifies AI responses as coming from "Cardynal AI" before any human handoff occurs.

Human Oversight

Every Cardynal deployment includes human escalation. AI handles routine inquiries; humans handle exceptions. You control the escalation rules through playbooks.

Escalations include full conversation history and AI-generated summaries so your team has complete context.

Limitations

AI can make mistakes. While our agents are highly capable, they may occasionally:
  • Misunderstand context or nuance
  • Provide incomplete information
  • Fail to detect when escalation is needed

That's why human oversight is built into every deployment. We recommend regular review of AI conversations, especially during initial deployment.

Accuracy and Improvement

We continuously monitor AI performance and update our systems to improve accuracy. You can provide feedback on AI responses directly in the dashboard.

AI responses are generated based on:

  • Your knowledge base content
  • Conversation history
  • Configured playbooks and rules

The quality of AI responses depends significantly on the quality of your knowledge base.

Compliance

Cardynal operates in accordance with Israel's Protection of Privacy Law (Amendment 13, 2025) and follows the Privacy Protection Authority's guidelines on AI systems.

We implement:

  • Data minimization — only processing data necessary for the service
  • Transparency — clear disclosure of AI use to end users
  • User rights — access, correction, and deletion capabilities
  • Accountability — logging and audit trails for AI decisions
  • Bias monitoring — regular review of AI outputs

Third-Party AI Providers

We use commercial LLM APIs to power our AI. These providers:

  • Process data only to generate responses
  • Do not retain conversation data for training
  • Are bound by data processing agreements
