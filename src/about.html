Our Story

Years of working with customer service platforms — Intercom, Zendesk, local solutions — and always the same problems: too expensive, months to implement, or Hebrew that sounds like Google Translate. So we built something different.

Hebrew isn't a translated language

We built Cardynal from day one for the Israeli market. Natural Hebrew, proper RTL, and a tone that actually speaks to your customers.
